# Research

This directory holds planning, review, and decision artifacts for the TinyAya
TTS Lab.

Use it for:
- campaign plans
- architecture notes
- scorecards and review memos
- experiment decision records

Separation of concerns:
- `recipes/` defines runnable configs
- `experiments/` records executed runs
- `research/` captures hypotheses, comparisons, and decisions

Keep new research artifacts here instead of mixing them into launcher scripts or
experiment logs.
